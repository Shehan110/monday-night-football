# Privacy Policy – Monday Night Football

Last updated: 2026

Monday Night Football is a private football league management app used by players participating in weekly games in Croydon, South London.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect basic information provided by users such as:

* Player names
* Team selections
* Match statistics (goals, results, league tables)

This information is used only to manage the football league and display statistics within the app.

## How We Use Information

Information is used solely for:

* Managing teams and matches
* Displaying league tables and statistics
* Organising weekly football games

No personal information is sold, shared with advertisers, or used for marketing purposes.

## Data Storage

League information may be stored securely to allow the app to function and display match results and standings.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use standard internet services required to operate the application (such as secure network connections).
